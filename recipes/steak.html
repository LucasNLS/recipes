Steak

Description

Delicious beef with various flavors, Great for any lunch

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Spices

Instructions:

  1. Let the steak soak overnight with spices
  2. Get pan heated, and add butter
  3. Cook steak in pan until seared
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Enjoy!
