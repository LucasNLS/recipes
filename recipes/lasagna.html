Lasagna

Description

Delicious dish with a lot of healthy ingredients. Based from italy, you will not regret making this lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ham
  • Parsley
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the pasta
  2. Mix the tomato, and alfredo sauce
  3. Layer the pasta, ham, and sauce
  4. Put lasagna in the oven at 350 degrees for 35 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
