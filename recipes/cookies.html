Description

Delicious pastry with sweet and savory flavors. Perfect for any meal!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients
  2. Mix the wet ingredients
  3. Put dry ingredients in bowl of wet ingredients gradually
  4. Once dough is set, put chocolate chips
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 12 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
